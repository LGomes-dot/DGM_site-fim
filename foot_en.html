"Foot"

The sculpture 'Foot' was carried out as part of a project of the discipline Graphics, Movements and Interfaces of the Degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia of the School of Arts and Design of Caldas da Rainha. The objective was to help to better understand the structure of a human foot, so that the development of the remaining project could be carried out more easily and realistically.

Author: Adriana Barros
