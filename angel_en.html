"Angel"

We were told the need to create new characters for a new computer game, so we created several illustrations, one of them being Angel. This illustration was done within the discipline Illustration for Design, belonging to the Graphic Branch of the second year of the Degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia of the School of Arts and Design of Caldas da Rainha.
