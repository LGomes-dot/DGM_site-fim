"Verizon"

'Verizon’, a responsive website developed under the Responsive Web Design course for the second year of the Degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia at Caldas da Rainha School of Arts and Design. This is a website whose main goal is to help better organize our personal agenda, we can add places we would like to visit, restaurants we want to go to, professional projects to be developed, among others. This calculates how much time has been spent on each task, and allows you to create a plan for a full day, whether you are planning breaks during work in progress, or simply a day to unwind by going to places and doing different activities.
