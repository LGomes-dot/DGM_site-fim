"Movement"

Movement is a mobile application that lets you control all of our physical activity, regardless of whether we are exercising or simply hanging out with friends and family. This digital platform was developed under Responsive Web Design, chair of the second year of the Degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia of the School of Arts and Design of Caldas da Rainha.
