Model Stencil Fonts

This exhibition brings together the collection of Typography III (D.G.M) students' projects on the theme and technique of model stencil fonts. This show features promotional font posters, designs, work processes, font graphic applications through mockups related to ESAD.CR or the city of Caldas da Rainha. This project is the result of the students' first experiences in the field of digital font design.

The exhibition runs from 12 November to 24 November 2019 at ESAD.CR
