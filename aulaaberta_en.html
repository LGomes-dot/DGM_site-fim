Open Class width António Brito Guterres

António Brito Guterres, is a researcher in Urban Studies who has made social and cultural interventions in a variety of territorial realities in the context of the Lisbon Metropolitan Area. Last year he created with Vhils the play “6 de Maio” in an exercise to uncover prejudices about the diversity of stories that are part of the city's heritage, and in particular this neighborhood of Amadora, while alerting to autocratic urban transformation processes. and little participatory.

He is a member of Dinâmia´Cet ISCTE – IUL, a research center through which he participated in the Lisbon City Cultural Strategy, and is coordinator of social intervention projects for the Aga Khan Foundation. He was part of the URB team, which produced a set of fictional episodes on the outskirts of Lisbon.
