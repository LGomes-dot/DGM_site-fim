II Week of Employability of the Polytechnic of Leiria

After the success of the 1st edition, and in response to the expectations of students and companies, the Polytechnic of Leiria promotes the “II Week of Employability”, which runs from November 26 to 29 (in all schools of the institution) .

The program of activities includes actions on skills development, career counseling, skills revolution, talent management, entrepreneurship awareness, student testimonials and company and entity displays. With this initiative, the Polytechnic of Leiria intends to contribute to the professional insertion of students and graduates in the active life, as well as providing a privileged space for networking among the different labor market stakeholders.

The exhibition area is attended by 66 companies and entities, which will provide more than 1,000 job offers and internships in various areas taught by the Polytechnic of Leiria. The organization expects participation to exceed 700 students.
